ABOUT US

Zero Barefoot campaign is a non-profit and non-political organization, started locally and aims at achieving Zero barefoot in all primary schools in the republic of Kenya by the year 2030.

MISSION

Bringing Dignity to our schools.

VISION

To be a pillar of Hope to the hopeless and achieve Zero Barefoot in our Schools by 2030 in Kenya.
