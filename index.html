WHAT WE DO

Donating Shoes

At ZERO BAREFOOT we value children's wellbeing of getting quality education in line with SDG number 4, and it's our wish that by 2030 no school going pupil go to school barefoot. We distribute Shoes to the needy children mainly from public primary schools in Kenya under our mantra of "Bringing Dignity in our Schools". We believe every child has a right to be safe from hash conditions that come with walking barefoot.

Conserving Environment

Environment is a vital component of our existence.We therefore need a conserted,collaborative efforts to protect preserve,rehabilitate and Restore it at all costs.Besides providing resources to humanity,our very own life and existence is sustained virtually by the environment.Therefore when we visit every school to donate shoes we make sure that we plant atleast 500 trees seedlings in the school compound.

FEATURED PROJECTS

Zero Barefoot Launch

On 18th March,The Zero Barefoot campaign and Environment under the patronage of Ms Charlene Ruto in partnership with ELDOWAS and Uasin Gishu county government launched the Zerobarefoot campaign at Saramek Primary school,Soy constituency, donating 500 pairs of school shoes and 500 tree seedlings to the community of Saramek.
